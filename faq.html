Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some common questions about our disaster awareness platform:

  • How can I report a disaster?

    Users can submit reports by visiting the "User Reports" section and filling out the required details.

  • What types of disasters are covered?

    Our platform provides information on hurricanes, floods, earthquakes, tsunamis, and other natural disasters.

  • How do I access emergency contacts?

    Emergency contact numbers can be found in the "Emergency Contacts" section of the website.

  • Can I contribute real-time data?

    Yes! Our platform allows users to share real-time updates on disasters.
