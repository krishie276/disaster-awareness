Disaster Preparedness & Safety Guides

Hurricanes

A powerful tropical storm with heavy rain

and strong winds.

Preparedness tips:

  • Secure outdoor objects
  • Stock emergency supplies(food,water,batteries)
  • Know you evacuation route
learn more

EarthQuake

Sudden shaking of the ground caused by

tectonic shifts.

Preparedness tips:

  • Drop, cover and hold on during shaking
  • Identify safe spots(under sturdy funiture)
  • Prepare an emergency kit with food, water and first-aid
learn more

Hurricanes

Overflow of water onto dry land caused

by heavy rainfall or storm surges.

Preparedness tips:

  • Avoid walking or driving through flood waters
  • Elevate electrical appliances
  • Have a family evacuation plan
learn more

Wildfires

A fast-spreading, uncontrolled fire that burns in forests, grasslands, or other natural areas,

often caused by dry conditions, lightning, or human activity.

Preparedness tips:

  • Secure outdoor objects.
  • Stock emergency supplies (food, water, batteries).
  • Know you evacuation route
learn more

Tsunami

A series of large ocean waves caused by underwater earthquakes,

volcanic eruptions, or landslides.

Preparedness tips:

  • identify safe evacuation routes and locations
  • Make a disaster kit with essential supplies.
